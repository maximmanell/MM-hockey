HC TPS – Turku, Finland

HC TPS (Hockey Club TPS) is a historic ice hockey team based in Turku, Finland. Competing in Liiga, TPS is known for their black-and-white jerseys and elite player development.

  • Founded: 1922
  • Home Arena: Gatorade Center
  • Finnish Championships: 11
  • Notable Players: Saku Koivu, Miikka Kiprusoff, Mikko Rantanen

Tornado Luxembourg – Kockelscheuer, Luxembourg

Tornado Luxembourg is the oldest and most successful ice hockey club in Luxembourg. The team plays in the French ice hockey system and represents the country at a competitive level.

  • Founded: 1971
  • Home Arena: Patinoire de Kockelscheuer
  • League: French Division 3
  • Colors: Yellow and Blue
  • Known for promoting Luxembourgish talent and international players
